## Content Insights
From the LinkedIn Learning video and the Jekyll documentation I learned that there are a wide variety of CMSs which work in very different ways. I was intially under hte impression that they were all bloated, slow, overbearing systems that were only useful for managing dizzying amounts of information, but I have since learned that there are many which work well even for small sites and which are lean and performant.

## Career or Employability Insights
There are plenty of CMSs out in the wild, but they all boil down to `data + presentation = content`, where you supply the data and (at least some of) the presentation, and the CMS presents the content.
